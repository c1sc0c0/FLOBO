Cobra.
(Bhujangasana)

• Lie face down, brow on the floor or mat. Keep your buttocks firm and feet close together. Put palms flat on the floor under your shoulders.
• Inhale; curl your upper body as you slowly raise your brow, nose, chin, shoulders, and chest. Pelvis remains on the floor.
• Relax shoulders and arms. Breathe.
• Slowly lower your body: waist, chest, chin, nose, brow.
• Pause and repeat.

I rise to meet Life's challenges with calm and confidence.

Benefits:.
• Tones the back and buttocks.
• Expands the rib cage, chest, and abdomen.
• Firms the throat.
• Replenishes the lower back and pelvis with a fresh supply of blood and increases circulation.
