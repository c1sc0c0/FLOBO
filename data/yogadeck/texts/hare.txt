Hare.
(Sasangasana)

• Sit in Child pose.
• Bend forward, placing the crown of your head on the floor.
• Lift your buttocks and grasp your heels with your hands.
• Feel the stretch along the shoulders and upper back.
• Hold for as long as is comfortable.
• Return to Child pose.

I give myself permission to go within and reenergize.

Benefits:.
• Reverses the force of gravity.
• Brings a fresh supply of blood to the brain, and to the pituitary and thyroid glands.
• Improves the complexion.

NOTE: Skip this pose if you have glaucoma or high blood pressure.
