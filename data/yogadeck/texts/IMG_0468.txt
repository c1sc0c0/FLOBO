(12) Downward Dog
(Mukhasana)
(fig. 1):
• Begin in "table position," with arms under, shoulders
and legs under hips.
o (fig. 2):
• Tuck toes under, exhale, and straighten, your legs.
Visualize your tailbone pointing toward the ceiling.
* Push your palms down, straighten your back. Head
hangs.down.
• Place your feet flat on the floor by picking up one heel
and lowering the other; alternate for several breaths.
I enjoy freedom of movement,
flexibility of thought.
Benefits:
• Aligns the spinal column
• Releases tension in the shoulders
• Stretches and lengthens the hamstrings
• Lengthens and increases flexibility in the Achilles
tendons
