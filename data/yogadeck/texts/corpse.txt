Corpse.
(Shavasana)

• Lie flat on your back.
• Keep legs about a foot apart. If the small of your back is not flat on the floor, place a bolster or rolled blanket under your knees.
• Keep arms by your sides, palms up.
• Close your eyes.
• Breathe deeply.
• Relax.

My body is rested. My mind is calm. My heart is at peace. My spirit soars.

Benefits:.
• Used between postures, this resting place allows the body and mind to absorb the benefits of the previous pose. From this center of rest and relaxation, you will be well equipped to face the day.

NOTE: The Corpse is an excellent pose from which to perform either the.
Complete Breath or the Sounding Breath.
