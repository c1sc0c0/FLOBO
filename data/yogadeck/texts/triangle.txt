Triangle.
(Trikonasana)

• Stand with feet about three feet apart.
• Turn right foot out 90'; turn left foot in about 30°.
• Inhale, stretch arms out from shoulders. Hips forward. Bend right.
• Rest right arm on shin and extend your other arm toward the ceiling. Look up at your thumb it you can do so without straining:.
• Hold for several breaths.
• Release, rest, reverse pose.

I am balanced and secure, open to new possibilities.

Benefits:.
• Improves flexibility of the torso.
• Strengthens the feet, ankles, and legs.
• Elongates the spine.
• Opens the hip area.
• Increases endurance and stamina.
