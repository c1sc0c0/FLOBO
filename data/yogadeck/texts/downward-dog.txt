Downward Dog.
(Mukhasana)

• Begin in "table position," with arms under, shoulders and legs under hips.
• Tuck toes under, exhale, and straighten, your legs.
• Visualize your tailbone pointing toward the ceiling.
• Push your palms down, straighten your back. Head hangs down.
• Place your feet flat on the floor by picking up one heel and lowering the other; alternate for several breaths.

I enjoy freedom of movement, flexibility of thought.

Benefits:.
• Aligns the spinal column.
• Releases tension in the shoulders.
• Stretches and lengthens the hamstrings.
• Lengthens and increases flexibility in the Achilles tendons.
