Hero
(Virasana)
Sit with your back straight. Cross legs so that right
knee is on top of left knee.
• Move feet away from hips so that you feel a hip
stretch
• Place palms of hands on the soles of your feet.
• Bend your upper body forward onto knees.
• Relax and breathe slowly.
• Return to upright seated position and repeat with left
knee on right knee.
Reflect on the question;
"Who is a Hero?"
Benefits:
• Opens hips and hip joints
• Keeps knees flexible
• Massages inner organs
• Helps reduce any stiffness in the neck
• Calms and relaxes
