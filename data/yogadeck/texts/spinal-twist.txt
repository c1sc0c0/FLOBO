Spinal Twist.
(Matsyendrasana)

• Sit erect, legs extended.
• Fold your right leg; cross your left foot over your right thigh at midpoint.
• Inhale; bring right arm around the outside of left knee. Grasp right knee or instep of right foot.
• Exhale; rotate your trunk to the left. Keep shoulders level.
• Raise left arm up, around, and back, placing palm on the floor. Turn your head, gaze over left shoulder. Hold for one minute.
• Release; repeat on right side.left stretchy.
