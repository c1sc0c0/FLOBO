Through-the-Hole Stretch
• Lie on your back with both knees bent, head on the
floor.

• Cross your left leg over your right thigh. Center ankle
bone on right knee. Bring your left arm through the
space between your legs and around right thigh. Clasp
hands.

• Draw right thigh toward your chest. Move left knee
away from your body.
• Hold for several breaths. As you exhale, draw right
thigh slightly closer to your body.
• Unclasp hands; undo legs. Repeat on the other side.
