Sitting Forward Bend.
(Paschimottanasana)

• Sit erect, legs straight, pelvis tipped forward, feet perpendicular to legs.
• Inhale, raise your arms straight ahead; exhale, stretch forward and down. Keep back straight.

• Place hands comfortably under legs, whether under knees, calves, or ankles. Relax, allowing chest to sink toward thighs without arching your back. Look forward and out.
• Breathe softly. No straining!

I let go... into the Eternal Now.

Benefits:.
• Gives an intense stretch to the entire back.
• Massages the abdominal organs.
• Increases flexibility of the spine.
• Fosters a sense of calm and letting go.
