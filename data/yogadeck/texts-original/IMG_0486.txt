Navasana
Lie face down, chin on floor, arms stretched out in
tront.
fig. 1)
Exhale. Inhale and raise arms and legs at the same
time. Your lower abdomen presses into the floor.
• Hold and breathe.
• Release and lower.
(fig. 2):
• Repeat the pose a second time with your arms behind
your bäck, fingers interlaced.
• Hold and breathe.
• Release. Rest with your head turned to one side.
I am open to the ebb
and flow of Life.
Benefits:
• Strengthens the arms and back muscles
• Brings a fresh supply of blood to the lower organs
• Rejuvenates the body
